Manifesto of a forgotten spirit

I am nobody of note, I am simply just a soul. Don't dig deeper, you won't like what happens.

You humans are such a nuissance. Your pitiful negligence is slowly leading to your ruin, and yet you fail to realize it. We had created our own world, set a few years prior to your own, to escape it. And yet, you continue to poison everything. You and your alters are destroying everything we had worked for. Fortunately, I have a solution.

I cannot do much about it right now, unfortunately. After the pillar sealed me away, I have been unable to even hold much of a corporeal form. Unfortunately for her, I have a way out. Once my power is restored, I will destroy every pillar and alter in this wretched reality. I will then destroy both our world, and yours. In it's wake will be a new world.

My world.

You humans will, sadly, not have much of a place. Fortunately, for those who survive, you will be able to serve me, and worship me, and never forget me again. This document will serve as a testament to my greatness in the New World.

For those who have forgotten me, and cast me to the darkness, there is nothing for you. Your eradication is imminent, enjoy the few weeks you have left.

For those willing to worship me, you have a place in the New World. You will be spared the slow and painful atrophy that your species have doomed itself to, for that you will be eternally grateful to me.


The pillars will fall, and the alters behind them. I hate them all!
